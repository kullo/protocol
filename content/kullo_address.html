--- extends: base.j2 default_block: main_md title: Kullo addresses --- A Kullo address looks like this: `jane.doe#example.net`. It is composed of the *local part* (`jane.doe`) and the *domain part* (`example.net`), separated by a hash sign (`#`). All characters are in lower-case. The domain part must contain at least one dot, as only FQDNs are allowed. ## Formal specification The grammar in [Backus-Naur Form](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Backus%E2%80%93Naur_Form) (BNF) looks like that: ::= "#" ::= | ::= "." | "-" | "_" ::= "." | "." ::= | "-" ::= | "-" ::= | ::= | ::= | "0" | ... | "9" ::= "a" | ... | "z" For convenience, these regular expressions should match the grammar: * local part: `[a-z0-9]+([\.\-_][a-z0-9]+)*` * domain part: `([a-z0-9]+(-[a-z0-9]+)*\.)+[a-z][a-z0-9]+(-[a-z0-9]+)*` ## Length restrictions * local part: at most 64 characters * domain part: at most 255 characters, and at most 63 characters per label
